About me

My name is Paul Elkington and I am a motivated individual with a strong passion for success and an impressive ability to work well under pressure. I healed an Events Management Diploma from Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT) in Cape Town, it was here I discovered a passion for building events and witnessing the joy they bring to people. I currently work as a Senior Project Manager for the Ironman Group, and have accumulated over 14 years of experience. Throughout my career, I have honed my problem-solving skills and becoming adept at navigating the complexities of large-scale project management.

In addition to my professional accomplishments, I am an avid outdoor enthusiast. I enjoy surfing, trail running, and riding motorbikes. My hobbies provide a balance to a busy career, allowing me to recharge and stay inspired. I am dedicated to both my work and personal interests to live a dynamic approach to life as this allows me to continuously seek new challenges and experiences.

After leaving computer science behind for a couple of years, I have made the time to nurture my interest in coding and software development. Looking to the future, I am eager to transition into a career in software development. I am driven by the desire to build and solve problems, and I see the software industry as the perfect arena to apply my extensive knowledge while continuing to learn and grow. I am excited about the opportunity to develop software that is not only functional but also enhances the user experience, furthering my commitment to creating impactful and meaningful projects.
